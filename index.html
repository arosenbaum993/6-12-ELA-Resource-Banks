How to use these tools
Step 01
Open the resource bank for your grade. Type what your students are struggling with — or pick a standard chip.
Step 02
The AI interprets your request, extracts the standard, and generates 1–3 resources tagged by DOK and standard code.
Step 03
Each card includes a teacher resource, student-facing task, ALD proficiency target, and common instructional mistakes.
Step 04
Click Print to send directly to the printer, or Copy to paste into a doc. Ready to use tomorrow.
